Pathao · Search & Discovery · 2024
Case Study 02

Intent-Weighted Search Ranking at Scale

The Problem
Search was the highest-intent entry point on the app — but users were dropping off due to typo sensitivity and a keyword-match model that couldn't read intent. A query like "biryani near me" returned restaurants that literally had "biryani near me" in their name.
What I Did
Audited search drop-off funnels to isolate typo, zero-result, and irrelevant-result failure modes
Rebuilt ranking with intent-weighted signals — category, cuisine, proximity, and popularity combined
Implemented fuzzy matching and query correction to absorb typo drop-offs
Shipped iteratively with holdout groups to measure incremental CR lift at each step

Pathao · Personalisation · 2023
Case Study 03

Behavioural Discovery — Time-Slot Modelling

The Problem
Discovery was static — every user saw the same ranked list regardless of when they opened the app or what they'd ordered before. Lunch users saw dinner-heavy results. Weekend orderers saw weekday restaurants first. No personalisation = no relevance = low CTR.
What I Did
Built a 6-month time-slot model segmenting user order history by day-part and day-of-week
Introduced proximity + recency + frequency signals into the "Order Again" surface
Built cross-area personalisation for users who frequently order outside their home zone (+8% new-area CVR)
Launched co-order recommendations increasing attach rate by 6% and AOV by 4%

Case Study 06 · 0→1 Product Build

Biniyog.com.bd — Bangladesh's All-in-One Retail Investment Platform

Context & Role
Biniyog is Bangladesh's most comprehensive retail investment platform — covering DSE stock trading, IPO management, bank schemes, Sanchayapatra, and tax tools in one place. I was the sole PM, building the entire product from concept through 500K+ active users over three years at SciTech Innovations.
Core Trading Platform
Designed and shipped end-to-end DSE stock trading: real-time portfolio tracking (P&L, dividends, bonus/rights), online buy/sell order execution, and live transaction status updates
Built BO (Beneficiary Owner) account opening and linking flows, online fund deposit/withdrawal via payment gateway — enabling fully paperless investing for retail users
Automated IPO application with active IPO listings, one-click applications, status tracking, and deadline notifications — eliminating broker visits for IPO participation
Research & Analytics Suite
Integrated TradingView technical analysis charts with customisable indicators, support/resistance levels, and historical price trend analysis
Built a fundamental + technical company screener filterable by P/E ratio, market cap, dividend yield, and sector — enabling data-driven stock selection
Portfolio analysis module showing cross-holding risk, sector allocation, total P&L, and Hold/Sell/Buy signals per position
Watchlist with customisable price-movement, volume, and market alerts — ensuring users never miss a key entry or exit signal
Paper Trading
Built a zero-risk simulated trading environment — a first for Bangladesh retail — letting users test strategies and build conviction before committing real capital
Multi-Asset Investment Hub
Bank scheme comparison across all major Bangladeshi banks with DPS/FDR interest calculator — graphical profit projection by tenure and rate
Sanchayapatra (government savings certificates) scheme directory with eligibility and return information
Tax rebate calculator: input income and investment breakdown to determine applicable rebates across all government-approved investment vehicles
Content & Education Engine
DSE market news feed sourced from DSE database and online press; weekly newsletter covering macro + market summaries — driving retention and return visits
Educational blog library covering fundamental analysis, technical patterns, and trading strategy — serving beginner to experienced investors and driving organic SEO acquisition
bKash super-app integration — extended Biniyog's reach inside Bangladesh's largest MFS app, opening a new high-intent distribution channel

Case Study 07 · 0→1 Product Build

MyClassroom.online — Next-Generation LMS for K-12 to Corporate

Context & Role
MyClassroom.online is a unified learning management platform built for schools (K-12), universities, and corporate training institutes. Designed to be simple, seamless, and scalable — no software installation required. I owned the full product lifecycle as PM, architecting a four-persona experience (students, teachers, parents, institutions) serving Bangladesh and international markets including Australia.
Virtual Classroom
Shipped live and recorded virtual classroom with real-time + async chat, group-based learning tools, live stream and lesson recording capabilities — enabling remote education with zero software installation
Automated attendance tracking built into the classroom layer — eliminating manual register management for teachers
Exam & Assessment Engine
Built a secure, time-bound online exam system with automated marking and grading — cutting manual evaluation hours to zero
Integrated question bank with algorithm-based random question generation per student, eliminating copy risk in online exams
Fraud detection capability built into the exam flow — preventing academic dishonesty in remote settings
Assignment Management
Designed time-bound assignment workflow with online submission, due-date enforcement, and engagement tracking — replacing all paper-based submission processes
In-class time-bound exercises for real-time student engagement during live sessions
Student Portfolio & Analytics
Shipped a performance analytics module giving teachers and parents real-time visibility into attendance, results, assignments, and engagement trends
Enabled personalised education interventions and student appreciation programmes based on analytics data
Virtual Library & LMS
Built a secured digital library supporting self-paced asynchronous learning with teacher-curated, filtered study materials and real-time content sharing for group and individual learning paths
Online Administration
Designed a centralised school operations layer: smart routine and schedule generator, intuitive admin dashboard for full operational visibility, and school fee collection management
Automated time-consuming manual administrative tasks — reducing institutional operational overhead significantly
Multi-Persona Architecture
Student: Single platform for all classes, resources, and assignments with real-time school environment navigation
Teacher: One platform to run online classes, manage assignments, run exams, track attendance, and assess students
Parent: Real-time tracking of tasks, results, attendance, and performance with direct teacher communication channel
Institution: Full operational management, teacher-student communication oversight, and administrative automation
